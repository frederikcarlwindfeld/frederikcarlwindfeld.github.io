About

Ph.D. Researcher at the European University Institute. My current research lies in the intersection between politics, security, and technology, and my interests include critical security studies, international political sociology, and the study of emergent and disruptive technologies in International Relations.

Research Interests

  • Politics, Security, and Technology
  • Critical Security Studies
  • International Political Sociology
  • Emergent and Disruptive Technologies
  • International Relations
